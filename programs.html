Master in Islamic Banking & Finance

A comprehensive 18-month postgraduate program covering Shari'ah-compliant banking products, sukuk structures, takaful, and modern Islamic capital markets. Graduates are equipped to lead in dual banking systems.

  • Covers conventional & Islamic finance
  • Capstone project in final year
  • Industry guest lectures

  • CPD credits awarded
