Featured Programs

Our flagship programs are designed for professionals and graduates seeking world-class education in finance and banking.

Postgraduate

Master in Islamic Banking & Finance

A comprehensive 18-month postgraduate program covering Shari'ah-compliant banking products, sukuk structures, takaful, and modern Islamic capital markets. Graduates are equipped to lead in dual banking systems.

  • 18-month intensive curriculum
  • AAOIFI-aligned syllabus
  • Research thesis required
  • Internship placement support
Apply Now
Undergraduate

BSc in Banking and Finance

A four-year undergraduate degree that builds a strong foundation in financial theory, banking operations, credit analysis, and regulatory frameworks. Ideal for students starting their career in the financial sector.

  • 4-year full-time program
  • Covers conventional & Islamic finance
  • Capstone project in final year
  • Industry guest lectures
Apply Now
Advanced Diploma

Advanced Diploma in FinTech

An industry-focused program exploring digital banking, mobile money ecosystems, blockchain applications, and AI-driven financial services — engineered for the future of banking in Africa.

  • 12-month accelerated format
  • Digital banking lab access
  • Partnership with tech companies
  • Portfolio-based assessment
Apply Now
Executive

MBA in Financial Management

Designed for mid-career professionals, this executive MBA blends strategic leadership with advanced financial management. Covers corporate finance, mergers, risk governance, and portfolio management.

  • Weekend/evening classes available
  • 2-year executive format
  • Case study methodology
  • Networking with industry leaders
Apply Now
Diploma

Diploma in Risk Management

A focused 12-month diploma that prepares students for roles in enterprise risk management, credit risk, operational risk, and regulatory compliance within banking institutions.

  • Basel III framework coverage
  • Practical risk modeling exercises
  • Industry case studies
  • Certification-ready curriculum
Apply Now
Certification

Certified Islamic Professional Accountant (CIPA)

An internationally recognized certification program aligned with AAOIFI standards. Covers Islamic accounting, auditing, governance, and ethics for financial institutions operating under Shari'ah principles.

  • AAOIFI internationally recognized
  • Online + in-person hybrid
  • Exam preparation included
  • CPD credits awarded
Apply Now
