About SLIBF

The Somaliland Institute of Banking and Finance (SLIBF) is a semi-governmental educational institution affiliated with the Central Bank of Somaliland and accredited by the National Commission for Higher Education. We are specialized in providing internationally accredited higher education and customized training programs in the field of banking and finance.
