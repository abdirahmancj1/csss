Institutional Background

The Somaliland Institute of Banking and Finance (SLIBF) is a semi-governmental educational institution affiliated with the Central Bank of Somaliland and accredited by the National Commission for Higher Education of the Republic of Somaliland.


The SLIBF is specialized in providing internationally accredited higher education and customized training programs in the field of banking and finance while providing a specialized scientific environment in this field using a method that takes into account the international standards in education and training according to comprehensive quality standards, ensuring to produce highly competent qualified individuals.


The main objective of the Institute is to respond to the educational and training needs in teaching, training and learning of the society of the Republic of Somaliland and the communities of the Horn of Africa. It also aims to develop the talents of workers in the banking and financial sector by providing high-quality educational and training programs that contribute to raising their scientific and professional levels.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

The SLIBF is an educational and training institution that intends to achieve excellence and leadership in education, training and applied practices in the field of banking and finance at the postgraduate level; to prepare specialized and qualified cadres in Islamic finance industry that contribute to the development of the financial sector for the societies of the Horn of Africa in general, and Somaliland in particular.

Our Mission

The SLIBF is to provide a specialized scientific environment in the field of banking and finance and to follow an approach that takes into account the originality of education and training according to quality standards to produce qualified cadres with high scientific competencies and good professional ethics that contribute to the economic and social progress of the societies of the Horn of Africa in general, and Somaliland society in particular.

Our Core Values

The members of the Institute — including students, professors, and employees — are committed to a set of values consistent with the Islamic religion.

Academic Quality & Professional Leadership

Upholding the highest standards in teaching, research, and professional development across all programs.

Transparency & Good Governance

Operating with integrity, openness, and accountability in all institutional affairs and decision-making processes.

Responsibility & Initiative in Teamwork

Fostering a collaborative environment where every member takes ownership of their role and contributes proactively.

Scientific Honesty & Professional Ethics

Maintaining the highest ethical standards in academic research, financial practice, and professional conduct.

Effectiveness & Efficiency

Ensuring excellence and measurable outcomes in teaching, training, and learning methodologies.

Respect for Law & National Systems

Upholding the regulatory frameworks, laws, and governance systems of the Republic of Somaliland.

Institutional Objectives

Our strategic objectives guide every academic and professional initiative at SLIBF.

  • 1

    Knowledge & Professional Skills

    Providing learners with knowledge and professional skills in the banking and financial sector.

  • 2

    Research Capabilities

    Building the capacity of the learners' practical and research capabilities in a unique and distinguished manner.

  • 3

    Leadership Development

    Preparing leaders of the banking and financial sector to reach their educational and training potential with integrity and high competency nationally, regionally and internationally.

  • 4

    Community Awareness

    Spreading practical and theoretical aspects in banking and financial awareness within the local community.

  • 5

    Islamic Ethics in Finance

    Paying attention to banking and financial studies and scientific research by routing and linking to Islamic ethics and principles.

  • 6

    Global Partnerships

    Building joint working agreements with reputable local, regional and international partners to achieve academic and professional excellence.

Accreditation & Affiliation

SLIBF operates under the oversight of the Central Bank of Somaliland and is accredited by the National Commission for Higher Education of the Republic of Somaliland.


Our programs follow international standards set by leading global bodies, ensuring our graduates are recognized and respected worldwide.
